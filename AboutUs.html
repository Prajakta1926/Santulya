About Us

Hey there! We're Santulya — where your health journey finds its balance.

Ever felt like no one really gets what it's like to deal with PCOD and PCOS as a teenager? You're not alone. We're a team of four women who've been there, and we created Santulya because we believe every girl deserves answers, support, and a community that truly understands.

What makes us different? We don't just talk about hormonal health — we live it. Our platform brings together everything you need: yoga sessions that actually work for your body, straight-talking doctors who listen, counselors who won't judge, and nutrition plans that feel empowering — not punishing.

Think of Santulya as your health bestie — the friend who shows up with solutions instead of just sympathy. Because in a world full of conflicting advice and empty promises, we believe you deserve better. You deserve balance. You deserve Santulya.

At the end of the day, you matter — and your health journey should feel as unique as you are.
